Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 20, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (optional)
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Device information for app functionality

Warranty and Receipt Data

To provide our core service, we collect and process:

  • Photos of receipts and warranty cards you upload
  • Extracted text and data from OCR processing
  • Purchase dates, vendor information, and product details
  • Warranty expiration dates and terms

Email Integration Data

With your explicit consent, we may access:

  • Email receipts from your inbox
  • Purchase confirmations and warranty information
  • Only emails related to purchases and warranties

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Core Services: Process warranty documents, extract information, and store your data securely
  • Send Notifications: Alert you before warranty expiration dates
  • Improve OCR Accuracy: Enhance our text recognition algorithms
  • Customer Support: Help you with app-related issues
  • App Analytics: Understand how users interact with our app (anonymized data only)

3. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement multiple layers of protection:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure Storage: Your documents are stored on secure, industry-standard cloud servers
  • Access Controls: Strict employee access controls and authentication
  • Regular Audits: Regular security assessments and updates
  • Data Backup: Secure backup systems to prevent data loss

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

Service Providers

  • OCR processing services (for text extraction)
  • Cloud storage providers (for secure data storage)
  • Email service providers (for notifications)
  • Analytics services (anonymized data only)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Data Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access all your stored warranty information anytime
  • Edit Information: Update or correct your warranty details
  • Delete Data: Remove specific warranties or your entire account
  • Export Data: Download your warranty information in a portable format

Communication Preferences

  • Control notification settings for warranty reminders
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Manage email integration permissions

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Needed to provide services (e.g., warranty tracking)
  • Required by law or for legitimate business purposes

When you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except for information we're required to retain by law.

7. Children's Privacy

TrackWarranty is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and provide adequate protection for your personal data.

9. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those third-party services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you use.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Sending an email notification
  • Posting a notice in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: trackwarrantyapp@gmail.com

Data Protection Officer: trackwarrantyapp@gmail.com

Website: trackwarranty.app
